Listening Technique

Listening is something that you can practice while walking , running or having fun so finding time to practice is not a problem and The key here is again : Comprehensible input so don't lose your time watching an american series on Netflix from which you cannot understand at least 70% of what is going on . With this said , the same technique for reading we are gonna apply here . Following along with the gradual process below there are resources and tasks that you can do to enhance your listening skills from A1 to B2
