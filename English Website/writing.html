Writing Technique

Here, I recommend that you start writing seriously if you are already at a B1 level. If you are not at this level, your vocabulary may be somewhat limited, making it difficult to develop your ideas effectively. While studying English, you will naturally practice your writing skills, but it's always beneficial to receive structured guidance on how to write properly in English. There is a playlist for IELTS Writing that, if you follow and complete the tasks along with the videos, will significantly improve your writing skills.
