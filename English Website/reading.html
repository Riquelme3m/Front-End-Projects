Graded Readers

Graded Readers is a technique where you progress from books at the lowest level (A1) to more difficult books (B2/C1, etc.). The objective is to advance step by step and improve gradually. One key aspect of learning any language is receiving COMPREHENSIBLE INPUT. For example, if you are watching a series or reading a book and cannot understand at least 60% of the content, you are not benefiting from it. This technique helps you level up gradually, so there are no surprises.

List of Tasks

Read 20 A1 books + Write down words and expressions you don't know in a personal notebook + Revise 4 times: the first one the day after reading, the second one in 3 days, the third one in 7 days, and the fourth one in 21 days.
