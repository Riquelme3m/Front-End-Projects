Grammar Technique

Grammar is, in my opinion, one of the most exciting aspects of learning a language. Understanding the structure of the language enables you to express a wide range of ideas, even if your vocabulary is not extensive. Therefore, as soon as you learn something new, you should incorporate it into your spoken English and revise it multiple times. For revision, you can use the spaced repetition technique recommended in the vocabulary section.
